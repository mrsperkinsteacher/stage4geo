About this unit. We investigate the invisible web of people, places and environments behind everything we use — how travel, technology and trade connect us; who wins and who loses in global trade; and how production and consumption can be managed more justly and sustainably. Along the way we build geography skills: maps, graphs, fieldwork and data analysis.
Big questions this term: Who made the things I use — and what is their life like? What does loving my neighbour look like in a global economy?
